ABOUT US

Our Story

Albertinia Pavers is a locally owned company specializing in manufacturing high-quality paving and construction materials. Established in Albertinia, Western Cape, our company was founded with the vision of supplying durable, cost-effective paving solutions to builders, contractors, and homeowners. We have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and affordability.

Our Commitment

At Albertinia Pavers, we believe in long-lasting quality, professional service, and customer satisfaction. Whether you need interlocking paving, retaining blocks, or cement bricks, we have the right products for your project.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our comprehensive services, covering both material supply and professional installation, proudly extend across the Western Cape. We serve Albertinia, Mossel Bay, Gourits, Boggomsbaai, Vleesbaai, Stilbaai, Riversdale, Heidelberg, Witsand, and all surrounding areas within approximately a 100km radius.
We are committed to providing you with the finest paving materials for your project, and we are proud to offer full professional installation services. While our expertise begins with the manufacturing and supply of materials, choosing our team to complete the installation ensures expert quality and a perfect, seamless finish from start to end.
Our products undergo certified laboratory testing for compressive strength (MPA) and fully comply with industry standards. Tests are performed by Roadlab in George..
