ABOUT US

Our Story

Albertinia Pavers is a locally owned company specializing in manufacturing high-quality paving and construction materials. Established in Albertinia, Western Cape, our company was founded with the vision of supplying durable, cost-effective paving solutions to builders, contractors, and homeowners. We have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and affordability.

OUR PRODUCTS

Bevel Pavers

Premium quality bevel pavers designed for superior performance and aesthetics. Perfect for driveways, walkways, patios, and pool decks. Their unique edge design provides excellent stability and creates beautiful patterns that enhance any outdoor space.

Cement Bricks

Our cement bricks are designed to offer strength and durability for construction projects. Ideal for building walls, foundations, and other structural elements, they are made from high-quality materials to ensure long-lasting results.

Interlocking Paving

Our interlocking paving bricks and coloured cement bricks are perfect for creating durable and stylish outdoor surfaces. Available in various colours and shapes, they are ideal for driveways, walkways, patios, and more. With a strong interlocking design, they provide stability, ensuring that your outdoor spaces remain intact for years to come.

Retaining Blocks

Retaining blocks are essential for creating strong and stable retaining walls. These blocks are designed to withstand pressure from soil and water, making them perfect for landscaping, erosion control, and garden design. They can even be used to create steps.

Building Blocks

Our building blocks are versatile, strong, and cost-effective. Suitable for a wide range of construction applications, including wall building and foundation work, they provide solid support for any project.
